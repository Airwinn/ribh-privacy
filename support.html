Common topics

  • Getting started: the app works fully offline — log what you buy and what you sell, and Ribh shows your real monthly profit.
  • Subscriptions (Pro / Expert): manage or cancel anytime in iPhone Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions. Tap Restore Purchases on the paywall to recover a plan on a new device.
  • Your data: everything is stored on your device. Cloud backup and multi-device sync are optional Pro/Expert features.
  • Delete your account & data: Settings → Account inside the app, or email us and we'll remove it.
